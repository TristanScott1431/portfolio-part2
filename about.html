When I'm not helping teams be productive, I'm typically doing the following things

Fishing

I spend alot of my free time fishing. I have a local wildlife reserve nearby with multiple ponds and a lake. I prefer pond fishing over lake fishing because it is easier to find the fish.

I typically go for largemouth bass. They are aggressive predators and they get big. That makes them especially fun to catch vs other fish. They are also plentiful, a huge variety of fresh waters have them, from big clear lakes to small murky ponds. You can catch them in Texas all the up to Canada.

Sim Racing

I also spend alot of my free time sim racing. Being a pro driver is my dream. Sim racing is a cheap and safe way I can experience that dream.

I'm normally playing F1 23, Assetto Corsa Competizione, or World Rally Championship.

Basketball

When I'm not fishing or sim racing you can find me in my local gym playing basketball

As well as being a great way to stay fit, basketball can also help you make new friends and teach you teamwork
